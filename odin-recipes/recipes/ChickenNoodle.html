Easy Chicken Noodle Casserole

Description

This chicken noodle casserole is super easy to prep with cooked shredded chicken, condensed soups, any noodles, and cheese. I'm sure that you will like it!!

The total time for it is only 40min with 10min for preparation and 30min for cooking,it can serve up to 6 people and u can feel free to add some toppings if you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken (3 pound) - cooked, deboned and shredded
  • 6 ounces spaghetti or egg nooodles
  • 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup (10.75 ounces)
  • 1 can condensed cream of chicken soup (10.75)
  • ¼ cup water
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  • Step 1 : Gather the ingredients
  • Step 2 : Cook spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until it is al dente, about 8 to 10 minutes. Let it drain and set it aside
  • Step 3 : Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place shredded chicken, cooked spaghetti, condensed soups, water, salt, and pepper in a 9x13-inch baking dish; stir to combine
  • Step 4 : Sprinkle cheese on top of mixture and bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted and golden brown on top, about 20 to 25 minutes. Let cool briefly before serving
